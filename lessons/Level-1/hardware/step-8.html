Core 1 Exam Overview

Understanding the exam structure

  • The first 4-5 questions will be scenario or performance-based questions; all others are multiple choice

Performance-Based Questions (PBQs)

  • Simulated scenarios requiring hands-on solutions
  • Usually appear at the beginning of the exam
  • Worth more points than multiple-choice questions
  • Strategy: Skip initially and return after MCQs if time permits

Multiple Choice Questions

  • Single answer vs. multiple correct answers
  • Situational scenarios
  • Direct knowledge testing
  • Process of elimination is key

Exam Scheduling & Instructor Review

  • Ensure an instructor has reviewed your practice exam attempt and distributed a test voucher
  • ALL CompTIA PBQs and practice questions should be completed before scheduling a certification exam
  • Contact your instructor and confirm your testing date and information

Test-Taking Strategies

  • Time Management: Total time ÷ number of questions = time per question
  • Reserve 20% time for PBQs
  • Mark uncertain questions for review
  • Watch the clock at 30/60/90 minute checkpoints

Question Analysis

  1. Read the entire question
  2. Identify key words
  3. Eliminate obvious wrong answers
  4. Consider most practical solution
  5. Trust your first instinct
