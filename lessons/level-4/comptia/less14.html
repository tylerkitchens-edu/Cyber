Overview

This lesson summarizes cloud computing concepts including deployment models (public, private, hybrid, community), service models (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS), elasticity, scalability, and emerging cloud trends. Students learn to compare cloud strategies, select the best model for specific business needs, and understand security and cost considerations in cloud adoption.

Hands-on labs and scenario-based exercises reinforce real-world decision-making in cloud architecture and deployment.
