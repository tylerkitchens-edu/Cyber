Overview

In this lesson, you will explore the fundamental concepts of network topologies, understanding how devices are arranged and connected within a network. You will learn about physical and logical topologies, the differences between them, and how they impact network performance, fault tolerance, and scalability. These concepts provide the foundation for building and maintaining efficient network infrastructures in real-world environments.
