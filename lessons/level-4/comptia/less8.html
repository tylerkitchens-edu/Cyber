Overview

This lesson focuses on network management principles and practices, including monitoring network performance, identifying bottlenecks, implementing SNMP, and using logging and alert systems. Students also learn about configuration management, change management, and proactive troubleshooting to ensure network reliability and efficiency.

Hands-on practice, combined with participation in meetings and 1-on-1 support sessions, will strengthen understanding of network management best practices.
