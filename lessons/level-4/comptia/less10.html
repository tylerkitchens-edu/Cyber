Overview

In this lesson, students will learn to apply security features such as firewalls, VPNs, intrusion detection/prevention systems, access control policies, and encryption techniques. Practical exercises include configuring and monitoring these features to ensure secure network operations while protecting sensitive organizational data.

Hands-on practice and engagement with peers, instructors, and TAs will help reinforce these concepts and their application in real-world networks.
