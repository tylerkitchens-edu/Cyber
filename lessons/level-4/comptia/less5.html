Overview

In this lesson, you will explore advanced routing and switching concepts, including how to configure and troubleshoot routers, switches, and VLANs in enterprise networks. You will learn about static and dynamic routing protocols, routing tables, and the implementation of advanced switching features such as trunking, port aggregation, and Spanning Tree Protocol (STP).

Understanding these concepts is critical for maintaining network stability, optimizing traffic flow, and ensuring redundancy in modern enterprise environments.
