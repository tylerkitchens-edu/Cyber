Overview

Students will explore key application-layer services that support end-user functionality, including HTTP/HTTPS, FTP, SMTP, and DNS. The lesson also covers application delivery methods, service monitoring, load balancing, and troubleshooting application service issues.

Mastering application services ensures smooth operation of web, email, and other critical services across enterprise networks, while also improving troubleshooting efficiency.

Stay engaged with weekly meetings, peer collaboration, and 1-on-1 sessions to reinforce practical understanding.
