Overview

In this lesson, students will learn to implement core network services critical to enterprise environments, including DNS, DHCP, NAT, and IP addressing schemes. Topics also include configuring email servers, file sharing services, print services, and authentication methods such as RADIUS and TACACS+.

Understanding these services ensures network reliability, proper resource allocation, and secure communication across the organization.

If the material seems challenging or dense, reach out to peers, instructors, or TAs. Participation in weekly sessions and one-on-one meetings will help reinforce practical skills and comprehension.
