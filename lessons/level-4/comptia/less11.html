Overview

Students will explore the principles behind secure network design, including segmentation, VLAN implementation, firewall placement, secure topology planning, redundancy, and high availability. Lessons also cover compliance standards, risk management, and implementing security policies to mitigate threats across enterprise networks.

Engage in exercises, weekly meetings, and one-on-one support to reinforce the practical application of secure network design principles.
