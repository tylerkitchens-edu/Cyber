Overview

In this lesson, you will learn how to support cabling and physical network installations, including identifying different cable types, connectors, and tools used for installation and maintenance. You will gain practical knowledge about copper, fiber optic, and wireless cabling standards, as well as best practices for safely installing and managing physical network components in various environments.

Understanding how to properly install and maintain cabling infrastructure is essential for reliable network performance. You will explore structured cabling systems, patch panels, wiring diagrams, and the importance of documentation and labeling in network environments.
