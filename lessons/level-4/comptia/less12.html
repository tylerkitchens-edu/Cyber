Overview

Students will learn how to configure, secure, and troubleshoot wireless networks, including understanding Wi-Fi standards (802.11a/b/g/n/ac/ax), frequencies, SSIDs, channels, encryption (WPA2/WPA3), and authentication methods. Practical exercises include access point configuration, wireless site surveys, and performance optimization.

Hands-on labs and peer collaboration reinforce real-world skills for deploying reliable and secure wireless networks in enterprise and small business environments.
