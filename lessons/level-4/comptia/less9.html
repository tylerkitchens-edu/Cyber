Overview

Students will explore core network security concepts including firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems, VPNs, authentication methods, access control, and encryption. Topics also cover common threats such as malware, phishing, and DDoS attacks, as well as best practices for risk mitigation and securing enterprise networks.

Engagement with peers, instructors, and TAs is encouraged to fully grasp these security principles and their practical applications in real-world networks.
