Overview

This lesson introduces resiliency and site security strategies for protecting business operations. Students will learn about physical site security, environmental controls, redundancy, failover mechanisms, disaster recovery sites, and business continuity planning. Emphasis is placed on designing resilient infrastructures that maintain operations during adverse events and ensuring continuity of critical systems.
