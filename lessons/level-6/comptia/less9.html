Overview

In this lesson, students will evaluate network security capabilities by analyzing security devices, technologies, and policies. Topics include firewalls, IDS/IPS, network segmentation, VPNs, access control lists (ACLs), and monitoring systems. The focus is on assessing strengths and weaknesses in network defenses and implementing improvements to reduce risk and enhance resiliency.
