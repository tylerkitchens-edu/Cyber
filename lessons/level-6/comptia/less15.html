Overview

Students will learn the processes of risk management, including identifying, analyzing, and mitigating risks to organizational assets. This lesson covers quantitative and qualitative risk analysis methods, risk prioritization, and the implementation of controls to reduce the likelihood and impact of security incidents. NIST RMF standards and best practices will be introduced as part of the methodology.
