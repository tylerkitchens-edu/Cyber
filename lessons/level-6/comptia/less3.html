Overview

This lesson covers cryptographic solutions for securing data in transit and at rest. Students will learn about symmetric and asymmetric encryption, public key infrastructure (PKI), certificate authorities, hashing, digital signatures, and secure protocols such as TLS/SSL. You will also explore practical applications of encryption in network security, authentication, and data integrity, as well as key lifecycle management.
