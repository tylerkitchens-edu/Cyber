Overview

In this lesson, students will learn to identify and analyze indicators of malicious activity, including logs, alerts, network traffic anomalies, and other system signals. Students will understand how to differentiate between normal behavior and suspicious or malicious activity, and how to implement monitoring and detection strategies to respond effectively.
