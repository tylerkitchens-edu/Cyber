Overview

This lesson covers endpoint security strategies to protect devices such as workstations, laptops, mobile devices, and IoT endpoints. Students will learn about antivirus, antimalware, endpoint detection and response (EDR), patch management, encryption, and mobile device management (MDM). Emphasis is placed on evaluating endpoint security posture, hardening devices, and reducing the attack surface.
