Overview

In this lesson, you will explore the fundamental concepts of cybersecurity, forming the foundation for securing information systems. Key topics include the CIA triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability), authentication and authorization methods, and basic cryptography. You will also learn about symmetric and asymmetric encryption algorithms, and understand how these concepts are applied in real-world environments to protect data and prevent unauthorized access.
