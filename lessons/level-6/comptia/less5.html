Overview

Students will explore best practices for securing enterprise network architectures. Topics include network segmentation, firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems (IDS/IPS), demilitarized zones (DMZs), VPNs, and defense-in-depth strategies. The lesson emphasizes designing resilient networks that reduce vulnerabilities, improve monitoring, and enforce secure communication across enterprise infrastructures.
