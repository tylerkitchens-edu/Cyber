Overview

This lesson covers incident response planning and monitoring concepts. Students will learn about the incident response lifecycle: preparation, detection, analysis, containment, eradication, recovery, and lessons learned. Additional focus is placed on monitoring techniques, security information and event management (SIEM) tools, log analysis, and alerting systems to detect and respond to threats efficiently.
