Overview

In this lesson, you will compare various cybersecurity threat types, including malware, social engineering, and network-based attacks. You will explore how each threat operates, the vulnerabilities they exploit, and the potential impact on systems and organizations. Emphasis will be placed on understanding ransomware, viruses, spyware, worms, trojans, phishing, spear-phishing, DDoS attacks, and man-in-the-middle attacks.

Understanding these threats will help you evaluate risks, implement preventive measures, and improve overall security posture in enterprise and cloud environments.
